Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Notice for Juuranto Group Oy ("we," "us," or "our") describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:

  • Visit our website at https://crm.lejos.fi
  • Download and use our mobile application (Lejos CRM)
  • Engage with us in other related ways

Questions or concerns? Reading this Privacy Notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at automation@lejos.fi.

Summary of Key Points

What personal information do we process? We process personal information depending on how you interact with our Services. This may include identifiers from your Microsoft Entra ID account, GPS location, device information, and activity data you create while using the app.

Do we process any sensitive personal information? No. We do not process sensitive information such as racial or ethnic origin, sexual orientation, or religious beliefs.

Do we collect any information from third parties? No. All information is collected directly from you or automatically from your device.

How do we process your information? To provide and maintain the Services, authenticate users, improve functionality, ensure security, and comply with law. We process your information only when we have a valid legal reason.

With whom do we share your information? With trusted service providers who help us operate the Services (Microsoft, Sentry, Expo). We do not sell your personal information.

How do we keep your information safe? Through appropriate technical and organizational security measures, including encryption, secure authentication (Microsoft Entra ID), and access controls.

What are your rights? Under GDPR, you have rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. You can exercise these rights by contacting us.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Disclose to Us

We collect personal information that you provide to us or that is made available through your Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD) account when you sign in:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Job title
  • Username
  • Authentication data

We also collect activity data you create when using the Services, such as store visit records, activity notes, and photos related to your field sales duties.

Sensitive Information. We do not process sensitive personal information.

Mobile Application Data

If you use our mobile application, we may collect the following with your permission:

  • Geolocation Information. GPS location is used to suggest nearby stores and to associate activities with locations. You can disable location access in your device settings at any time.
  • Camera and Photo Library. Access is requested so you can attach photos to activities. You can revoke this access in your device settings.
  • Device Data. Device identifiers, operating system, version information, device model, and related technical data — primarily for troubleshooting, security, and crash reporting.

Information Automatically Collected

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use the Services:

  • Log and Usage Data. IP address, device information, browser type, activity timestamps, features used, and error reports.
  • Device Data. Device identifiers, operating system, hardware model, and application identification numbers.
  • Location Data. GPS coordinates when the mobile app is in use and location permission has been granted.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Services — including authenticating your access, enabling location-based store suggestions, and recording your activity data.
  • To respond to user inquiries — to help resolve issues you encounter while using the Services.
  • To send administrative information — such as notifications about changes to the Services, policies, or account status.
  • To protect the Services — to detect, prevent, and address security issues, fraud, and technical problems.
  • To identify usage trends — to understand how the Services are used and improve them.
  • To comply with legal obligations — when required by applicable law.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we rely on the following legal bases to process your personal information:

  • Performance of a Contract. Processing is necessary to provide the Services to you as part of your employment with Juuranto Group Oy.
  • Legitimate Interests. We process data to improve the Services, diagnose technical issues, ensure security, and prevent fraud, where these interests do not override your fundamental rights.
  • Legal Obligations. Processing may be required to comply with accounting, tax, or other legal obligations.
  • Consent. Where you have given consent for specific purposes (such as enabling GPS location access), you may withdraw consent at any time.

4. When and With Whom Do We Share Your Information?

We share personal information with trusted service providers who process data on our behalf to operate the Services:

  • Microsoft Corporation — cloud hosting (Azure), authentication (Microsoft Entra ID), and database services.
  • Sentry — error tracking and crash reporting for the mobile application.
  • Expo (Expo Application Services) — mobile application build and deployment platform.

We may also share information in the following situations:

  • Business Transfers. In connection with a merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or part of our business.
  • Legal Requirements. If required by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with advertisers or marketing partners.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use only strictly necessary cookies required for the Services to function, primarily for user authentication (Microsoft Entra ID session cookies). These cookies do not require consent under GDPR as they are essential for the operation of the Services.

We do not use advertising cookies, marketing cookies, or third-party tracking pixels.

6. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide the Services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account and activity data — for the duration of your employment with Juuranto Group Oy, and up to 24 months thereafter.
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data — up to 90 days.
  • Data required by law — retained for periods specified by applicable law (for example, accounting records).

When we no longer need to process your personal information, we will delete or anonymize it, or securely store it and isolate it from further processing until deletion is possible.

7. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information, including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Microsoft Entra ID authentication with multi-factor authentication support
  • JWT-based authorization on our backend services
  • Azure network access restrictions
  • Encrypted storage (Azure SQL)

However, no electronic transmission or storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. You should only access the Services within a secure environment.

8. Information From Minors

The Services are intended for employees of Juuranto Group Oy and are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age. If you become aware of any data we may have collected from a minor, please contact us at automation@lejos.fi.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR, if you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — to request a copy of your personal information
  • Right to rectification — to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to erasure — to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to restrict processing — to limit how we use your information
  • Right to data portability — to receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — to object to certain types of processing
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at automation@lejos.fi. We will respond within one month, as required by GDPR.

If you believe we have unlawfully processed your personal information, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi) or another EU supervisory authority.
